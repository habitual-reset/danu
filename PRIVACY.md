# Privacy Policy — DANU

**Last updated:** June 28, 2026

**Operator:** DANU (sole proprietorship — personal AI assistant project)  
**Contact:** journey@habitualreset.com

This Privacy Policy describes how DANU ("we," "us," or "the Service") handles information when you interact with the assistant by SMS or voice call.

## Scope

DANU is currently operated for **personal use by the owner** and **authorized users only** (phone numbers on an explicit allowlist). This is not a mass-marketing or lead-generation SMS program.

## Information We Collect

When you text or call the Service, we may collect:

- **Phone number** (caller ID / sender ID)
- **Message content** (SMS body or voice transcriptions)
- **Call metadata** (time, duration, carrier identifiers such as Twilio SIDs)
- **Conversation and memory data** generated to provide continuity across interactions
- **Technical logs** (webhook delivery, errors, timestamps) for reliability and security

We do **not** knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Receive and respond to your messages and calls
- Maintain conversational context and long-term memory (core product function)
- Operate, secure, and debug the Service
- Comply with applicable law and carrier requirements

We do **not** use your phone number or message content for third-party advertising or sell your personal information.

## Mobile Information Sharing

**Mobile phone numbers and SMS/voice content are not shared with third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes.**

We share data only with service providers required to operate the assistant, such as:

- **Twilio** — telephony (SMS and voice)
- **Cloud hosting / tunneling** — to receive webhooks during development or deployment
- **LLM provider(s)** — to generate responses (when configured by the operator)

These providers process data on our behalf to deliver the Service.

## Data Retention

- Conversation events and memory are retained to provide ongoing context until deleted by the operator or superseded by the memory system.
- Logs are retained for a limited period for troubleshooting, then rotated or deleted.
- You may request deletion of your data by contacting the operator.

## Security

We use allowlisted phone numbers, webhook signature verification, and environment-isolated secrets. No system is perfectly secure; we work to reduce risk through access controls and audit logging.

## Your Choices

- **Access** is limited to authorized phone numbers.
- **Opt out of SMS:** Reply **STOP** to any message. We will cease sending SMS to your number (standard carrier opt-out).
- **Help:** Reply **HELP** for assistance.
- **Voice:** End the call at any time.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the Service evolves (including if it becomes consumer-facing). The "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
